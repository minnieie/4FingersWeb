FOURTH ROCK FROM THE SUN

A DESERT WORLD

Mars is a dusty, cold, desert world with a very thin atmosphere. Billions of years ago, there was a strong evidence that Mars was wetter and warmer, with a thick atmosphere.

It is home to Valles Marineris, a canyon system that runs along the Martian equator, and Olympus Mons, the largest volcano in the solar system.
