The 4Fingers Project Office receives communications from students and space enthusiasts worldwide. We endeavor to provide timely information about our Mars simulation.

Mars Explorer is a Year 2.2 integrated project by 4Fingers that integrates Unity XR development, Firebase backend systems, 3D environment creation, and UX design. It is an educational Mars simulation designed to help students learn about rocks found on Mars, discover new scientific information, and explore Mars’s potential habitability.
